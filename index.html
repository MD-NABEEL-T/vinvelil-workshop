About the Workshop

Space is not just for scientists—it is for everyone. "Vinvelil Oru Payanam" is an exclusive, beginner-friendly online workshop designed to demystify the wonders of the universe.

For the first time, complex astronomical concepts and the physics of the cosmos are being broken down and taught entirely in Tamil. Whether you are a student dreaming of a career in aerospace or simply someone fascinated by the night sky, this session will give you a breathtaking new perspective on our place in the universe.

No prior technical knowledge is required. Just bring your curiosity.

Meet Your Speaker

Tamizh Amuthan

Chief Scientific Officer (CSO) & Astronomy Lead, IARRD

Leading the astronomy division at IARRD, Tamizh specializes in breaking down high-level astrophysics into engaging, easy-to-understand concepts. His session is designed to inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts.

About IARRD

Indian Astronomy Rocket Research and Development (IARRD) is a pioneering space-tech organization driven by a mission to democratize space and defense technologies, making aerospace education accessible and empowering future innovators.
